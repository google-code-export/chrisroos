Home - About the Public Swimming Pools (in the UK) project

This started out of the frustrating experience I had trying to find public swimming pools in London: I tried, and failed, on multiple occasions to find swimming pools in different parts of London.

I've started by adding the four swimming pools that I've used most often, with a view to adding more in the, near, future.

Feel free to join the public pools google group if you have any questions, pools you want added or maybe just a chat.

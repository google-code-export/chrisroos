Windows Adobe Acrobat Reader alternative

Posted by Chris Roos Sun, 13 Nov 2005 16:08:54

I just re-installed Windows XP on my laptop as it has been almost unusable for the last few months due to large amounts of software cruft. I had a need to read a .pdf and didn’t really want to install Adobe as it just feels a little too bloaty to me. A quick google turned this little app up. It’s small, feels faster than Acrobat Reader (although that may be to do with the re-install) and is free. So far so good.

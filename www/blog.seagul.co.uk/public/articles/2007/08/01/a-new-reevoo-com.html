A new reevoo.com

Posted by Chris Roos Wed, 01 Aug 2007 08:32:00

This is obviously completely optional but if anyone stumbles across this post, I’d really appreciate it if you’d spend a few minutes checking out the revised reevoo.com.

I don’t want to say too much as I want to influence any judgement. Just check it out and let me know what you think, either in the comments here or by email. Oh, and really don’t be scared to slag it off, praise it up or just sit on the fence – any opinion is welcome.

Oh, and in case it’s not obvious, I work at Reevoo.

    On the face of it, I rather like it. I can’t compare it to the old version because, I’m afraid, I never saw it.

Ah, I went to “what does this mean” and I understand it now. Maybe it could be clearer on the page?

    Look and feel seems pretty intuitive, which is nice. Here’s a funny little thing though – when I wanted to switch back to the site to look at something, I had a moment of trouble finding the tab in Firefox. I realised that there were two contributing factors:
    • first, I was on the FAQ, so the tab caption was “Frequently Ask…”
    • second, the icon was the blue-quotes thingy

    On the favicon idea, I was looking for something orange, which suggests that I immediately assigned mentally the white “revoo” on an orange background as the logo – I think I might have spotted a white “r” in an orange circle more easily.

    But I’m a nit-picky geek1 with time in his hands while I wait for my database server to stop being broken.
